The primary objective for this pick and place machine is to populate PCBs with high speed and accuracy. It will be a desktop PNP (i.e. small, but large enough for most PCBs), featuring a dual gantry system, and numerous toolheads. 
Currently, I'm heavily referencing the [LumenPnP](https://github.com/opulo-inc/lumenpnp) and other [OpenPnP](https://github.com/openpnp/openpnp) projects. 

This will be the first project I make of this complexity, so please bear with me, and if you see anything obviously wrong, let me know!

#### Personal Objectives:
- Improve my CAD skills
- Incorporate more plugins in my CAD workflow (Fusion 360 and KiCad)
- Learn high current routing and proper grounding techniques
  
## The HRS (Hardware Requirements Specification) - 4 Hours
This project is made in parallel with a school assignment, hence why I made a HRS, and why I will be making a PDD and HDD later on.
